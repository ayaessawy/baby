About Us

Welcome to Gravid, a company started by a mom, for moms! Our mission is to provide high-quality, pre-packed maternity hospital bags for expecting mamas and baby, so they can worry about one less thing during pregnancy. Let us pack your bag for you so you can relax and enjoy the journey. Shop our site to view the four different bundle options available for mom and baby. Snag a bag to become a Llama Mama yourself, or have the BEST gift at the next baby shower! .
